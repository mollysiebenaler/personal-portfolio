Marketing Institute: Bobbi-Toads

During the fall and spring semesters of my senior year, I worked as a marketing consultant for Bobbi-Toads, a small business that sells children's shoes and accessories. My team provided Bobbi-Toads with product mix, branding, and channel strategy guidelines. The recommendations were based off secondary research into the chilren's apparel market, the collegiate sportwear industry, its competitors, and a SWOT analysis, as well as primary research consisting of a survey that received over 1,000 respondents, a focus group with children, and interviews with parents.

Data Mining

Using the Kaggle Hotel Reservations dataset, I built a predictive model in Orange using a decision tree framework to providea a new strategic direction for the given hotel. The model determined the most important factors in whether a guest cancels their hotel room. The model determined that lead time, average price of hotel room, and market segment type are the three most important factors. A higher lead time, more expensive hotel room, and corporate groups make it most likely that the reservation will be cancelled.
